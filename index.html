TOKY Burgers was founded by four chefs named Tareq, Omar, Khaled and Yusuf. Each chef came from a differnt backgound and influenced the flavour of our well renowned burgers. All burgers are handcrafted by our chefs and are made with fresh products supplied by local farms throughtout the UAE. Our sauces and ingredients are freshly prepered by us without any preservatives. Our mission is to provide great burgers to everyone.
