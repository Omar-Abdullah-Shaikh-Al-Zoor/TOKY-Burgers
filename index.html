Who are we?

TOKY Burgers was founded by four chefs named Tareq, Omar, Khaled and Yusuf. Each chefs' unqiue backgound influenced the flavour of our well renowned burgers. All burgers are handcrafted by our chefs and are made with fresh products supplied by local farms throughtout the UAE, and all our special sauces and ingredients are freshly prepered by us without any preservatives to achieve our mission of providing the best burgers to everyone.
